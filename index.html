About Melting Waveforms

A label home for AI-generated music built to feel cinematic, modern, and alive.

Melting Waveforms is an evolving label concept where AI voices, neon visuals, and curated songcraft meet. Each artist has a distinct color identity, but they all share a foundation of EDM, synthwave, and synthpop.

Every sound starts with a human spark — an overheard phrase, a real feeling, a moment worth remembering. AI brings it to life. The result is something that sounds like real life feels.
