Melting Waveforms Artist

Wren Wyngate

Country Electronic • Dubstep • Alt Country

Wren Wyngate left at eighteen and never looked back. Raised in a small southern town where everybody knew your name and nobody could keep a secret, she packed what she could carry and drove toward something bigger.

She landed in Austin, Texas — a city that understood her immediately. Raw, fierce country-electronic with heavy dubstep influence and a natural country accent that never softened, Wren makes music that sounds like standing at the edge of town with your bags packed and not looking back.

She didn't leave alone. But she arrived on her own terms.
