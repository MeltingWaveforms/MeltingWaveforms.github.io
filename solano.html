Melting Waveforms Artist

Solano Reid

Reggae-EDM Fusion • Synthwave • Dancehall

Solano Reid grew up counting his blessings — and meaning it. Warm, grounded and impossible not to move to, his music brings reggae roots into the neon EDM world of Melting Waveforms in a way nobody else on the roster does.

His debut single Walk In My Shoes asks a simple question — what would you do if you didn't have what you have? No guilt, no lecture. Just genuine gratitude and an infectious beat that makes you feel it.

He's the only artist on this label that will make you dance before you've processed what he's saying.
