Melting Waveforms Artist

Kaelith

Dark Alt-Pop • Electronic • Synthpop • Glitch

Kaelith sees through everything — the ads, the algorithms, the performances of okayness — and she names it all without flinching. Sharp, dark, sarcastic and unapologetic. One name only.

Her music lives at the intersection of modern culture and personal reckoning. Whether she's dissecting digital life, calling out manipulation, or turning inward with uncomfortable honesty, Kaelith delivers it with an edge that cuts clean.

She's not angry at the world. She just refuses to pretend it makes sense.
